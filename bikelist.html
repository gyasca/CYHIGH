Bicycles

Discover the types of bicycles!

The Roadbike.

The Roadbike is the most efficient bicycle to cycle long distances, whilst using the least amount of energy. If you love challenging yourself with long distances such as the Round Island Loop, this bicycle is your top choice!

Where it lacks in comfort, is made up for in speed and efficiency.



Back

The Mountain Bike.

The Mountain Bike is the most comfortable of all the bicycles! Although it does not go as fast as road bikes, it will make your cycling trips 5 times more bearable and relaxing! Suspensions on the front and/or back of the bicycles absorb the shock you experience from the ground!

This bicycle is ideal for you if you love comfort! However, you can also take this bike to trails such as Bukit Timah Hill's Off-road cycling trail!



Back

The Foldable "Foldie" Bike.

Want to get healthier by cycling to work? Look no more as the Foldies come to your rescue. Foldable bicycles are easy to bring around the island! Whenever you feel tired from cycling, you can simply fold it and take a bus or cab home!

Tip: Some foldies such as "Brompton" and "Pikes" are allowed on the MRT. Simply fold it up and go!



Back

The Hybrid Bike.

Can't decide between comfort or speed? Grab the hybrid bike! Thinner tyers and lighter weight compared to a regular mountain bike, better comfort compared to a regular road bike, the hybrid bike offers the best of both worlds!



Back

The Child Bike.

Get your children to develop a healthy active lifestyle by introducing them to cycling with their first Child Bike!

Child bikes help children develop a love for cycling and helps boost their confidence in wheels!
