Interesting fact about Singapore's
cycling infrastructure

Singapore has one of the most robust cycling infrastructure in the world. Park connector networks make this possible. There is currently around 300km of park connector networks spanning all across Singapore. You could cycle around the whole country without cycling on a single road!
